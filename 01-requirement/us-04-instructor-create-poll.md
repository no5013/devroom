# US-04: Instructor Creates a Poll Question

**As an** instructor,
**I want to** post a question with predefined answer choices to the chat,
**So that** I can get quick structured feedback without waiting for people to type.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The instructor can open a "Create Poll" panel within the session
- [ ] The instructor enters a question text (e.g. "Is everyone ready to move on?")
- [ ] The instructor defines between 2 and 6 answer options (e.g. Yes / No / Need more time)
- [ ] Each answer option has a short label (≤ 30 characters)
- [ ] Posting the poll immediately broadcasts it to all connected participants
- [ ] The active poll is visually prominent — it appears above or overlays the chat so participants cannot miss it
- [ ] The instructor can close/dismiss a poll at any time, which hides it from participant view
- [ ] Only one poll can be active at a time per session
- [ ] Previously run polls and their final results are accessible in a history list for the instructor
