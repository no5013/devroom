# US-06: View Live Poll Results

**As an** instructor,
**I want to** see real-time aggregated results while a poll is running,
**So that** I can read the room and decide when to move on.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [ ] The instructor sees a live results view showing vote counts and percentages per option, updating in real time
- [ ] Results are displayed as a bar chart or progress bars so the distribution is instantly readable at a glance
- [ ] Total number of participants who have voted (at least once) is shown
- [ ] Results update with sub-second latency as participants spam their answers
- [ ] The instructor can optionally share the live results view to participants (toggle: show results to room / hide results from room)
- [ ] When the poll is closed, the final snapshot of results is frozen and saved to poll history
- [ ] The results view is readable on a projected screen (large text, high contrast)
