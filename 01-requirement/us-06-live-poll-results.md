# US-06: View Live Poll Results

**As an** instructor,
**I want to** see real-time aggregated results while a poll is running,
**So that** I can read the room and decide when to move on.

## Acceptance Criteria

- [x] The instructor sees a live results view showing vote counts and percentages per option, updating in real time
- [x] Results are displayed as a bar chart or progress bars so the distribution is instantly readable at a glance
- [x] Total number of participants who have voted (at least once) is shown
- [x] Results update with sub-second latency as participants spam their answers
- [x] The instructor can optionally share the live results view to participants (toggle: show results to room / hide results from room)
- [x] When the poll is closed, the final snapshot of results is frozen and saved to poll history
- [x] The results view is readable on a projected screen (large text, high contrast)
